Dr. James Kelvin, Lead Pastor

Our Lead Pastor

Dr. James Kelvin, "JK" as he is fondly called by his teeming followers worldwide is a Man of God seasoned in the Word, filled with faith, and anoited by the Holy Ghost with miracles, signs and wonders. Several people have been healed of diverse infirmities, and a few dead raised in his meetings.
He is highly sought after for conferences, conventions, prayer rallies, to mention but a few.
Happily married to Dr. (Mrs) Sonya Kelvin, he is blessed with two children, Joshua and Deborah.

World of Faith Ministries

Word of Faith Ministries is a church made up of people from many ethnic backgrounds and nationalities with thousands of worshippers in all her locations in different parts of the world.

Vision Statement

To be a people who serve God, love one another, and saturate their environment and world with tangible proofs of the presence and power of God through the force of faith

Mission Statement

To be a church that lives by faith, is known by love, and is a voice of hope to the world
